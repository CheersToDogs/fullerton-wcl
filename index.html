Redaction Review Authorization

The Remarks field contains the full narrative text of incident , as entered by the watch commander.

This field may contain information California law requires to be reviewed and redacted before public release, including:

  • Victim identifying information in sexual assault cases (PC §293)
  • Juvenile identifying information
  • Active investigation details (CPRA §6254)
  • Witness identifying information

This data is published by the City of Fullerton at a public ArcGIS endpoint without redaction review. This viewer surfaces the misconfiguration for responsible disclosure and redaction compliance.

By proceeding, you confirm you are authorized to view this field for purposes of redaction review, security research, or journalism.
